---

The Filming and Photography Policy Page provides information on the policies, procedures, and regulations applicable to individuals and organizations wishing to conduct photography, videography, content production, or filming in the Tempe Park area. This page aims to ensure that all documentation activities are conducted in an orderly, safe, and respectful manner, as well as in line with the values ​​and regulations applicable to Tempe Park.

<Frame>
  ![Photography Policy Cut](/images/Photography-Policy-Cut.png)
</Frame>

## Overview

- Displays information regarding policies and requirements for photography and videography activities in the Tempe Park area.
- Provides a guide to the approval process before carrying out photography activities.
- Displays rules regarding restricted area access, visitor privacy, and use of production equipment.
- Provides guidance on content standards, filming ethics, and the use of material for publication or commercial purposes.
- Displays information about prohibited activities, environmental responsibilities, and consequences for violating policies.
- Provides a Filming or Photography Request button to make it easier for users to apply for permission for photography or videography activities.
- Provides quick access to Ticket and Back to Top features to improve user convenience when reading pages.

## User Story

<Accordion title="View User Story" defaultOpen>
