## Overview

- Featuring the Experience page as a central point for exploring the various attractions, activities, and amenities available at Tempe Park.
- Provides a hero section with key visuals of the Tempe Park area to give users an initial overview of the experience offered.
- Displays category navigation in tab form (Attraction, Activities, Facilities) to make it easier for users to move between types of experiences.

## User Story

<Accordion title="View User Story" defaultOpen>
  - **As a user** who wants to know the experience at Tempe Park,
  - **I want to** see the various attractions, activities and facilities available on one page,
  - **So I can** understand the choice of experiences offered and determine which activities I want to participate in.
  - **As a user**,
  - **I want to** be able to move between categories (Attractions, Activities, Facilities) easily.
