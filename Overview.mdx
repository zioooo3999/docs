## 2.1 Background

Users have assessed that the current Tempe Park website **no longer meets modern user experience standards**. Key issues identified include:

<CardGroup cols={3}>
  <Card title="Unintuitive Interface" color="#ef4444" icon="hand-pointer">
    The existing interface lacks clarity and ease of use, making it difficult for visitors to navigate and find relevant information efficiently.
  </Card>

  <Card title="Poor Information Structure" color="#ef4444" icon="sitemap">
    The content architecture is suboptimal — sections are disorganized, making the overall flow of information hard to follow for new and returning visitors.
  </Card>

  <Card title="Outdated Visual Design" color="#ef4444" icon="palette">
    The visual design appears dated and out of step with current design trends, weakening Tempe Park's brand perception as a modern edu-park destination.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

Furthermore, **limitations in content management** within the existing system pose a challenge — particularly in terms of flexibility and efficiency in updating information. This results in suboptimal information delivery to visitors and potentially reduces user engagement.

<Note>
  To address these issues, the Tempe Park website was revamped with a modern approach — both in **technology** and **design**. The new website is built using a **CMS-based architecture**, utilizing **Strapi** as the backend for content management and **Next.js** as the frontend framework for optimal performance and a more interactive user experience.
</Note>

## 2.2 Objective

The development of the Tempe Park website aims to build a **centralized digital platform** capable of representing Tempe Park's identity, vision, and strategic values as an Edu-Park and innovation center.

This website is expected to function not only as an information medium, but also as a **strategic tool** to support various aspects of operations and business development.

Specifically, the objectives are:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Providing an Integrated & Credible Information Platform">
    Delivering complete, accurate, and always up-to-date information regarding existing programs, facilities, activities, and initiatives at Tempe Park.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Increase Brand Awareness & Positioning">
    Strengthening the image of **Tempe Park** as a center for education, innovation, and tempeh research at the national and international level.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Improve User Experience">
    Presenting a modern, intuitive, and responsive website appearance in accordance with the latest UI/UX standards — making it easier for users to access information and interact.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Supporting Public Education & Engagement">
    Serving as an educational medium for the public about tempeh, food innovation, and sustainability — while increasing user engagement through interactive content and activities.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Facilitating Visitor Journey Planning">
    Providing convenience for visitors in planning their trips through clear information regarding tickets, schedules, facilities, and available activities.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Supporting Collaboration & Business Opportunities">
    Opening up opportunities for collaboration with institutions, communities, and business partners through dedicated collaboration and contact features.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Improve Content Management Efficiency">
    With a **Strapi-based CMS**, internal teams can easily manage, update, and distribute content flexibly — without high dependence on technical teams.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Optimizing Platform Performance & Scalability">
    Using **Next.js** as the frontend, the website is built for high performance, strong SEO, and the ability to scale alongside future business needs.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## 2.3 Scope

The scope of the Tempe Park website development covers the following main aspects:

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Landing Page Development" color="#6366f1" icon="layout-grid">
    Website implementation based on the designed structure — including the main page and all derivative pages such as **Homepage, Plan Your Visit, Experience, Education, Innovation**, and other supporting pages according to the provided UI/UX design.
  </Card>

  <Card title="Frontend Implementation" color="#0ea5e9" icon="code">
    User interface development using **Next.js** with a focus on:

    - High performance
    - SEO-friendly output
    - Responsive design for desktop, tablet, and mobile
  </Card>

  <Card title="Backend Implementation (CMS)" color="#10b981" icon="server">
    The use of **Strapi** as a Content Management System to support flexible content management — including the creation of content types and setting roles & permissions for CMS users.
  </Card>

  <Card title="Content Management & Integration" color="#f59e0b" icon="plug">
    Integration between frontend and CMS via **API**, plus implementation of content management features such as articles, programs, activities, and multi-language support **(Indonesian, English, and Chinese)**.
  </Card>

  <Card title="Main Website Features" color="#8b5cf6" icon="star">
    Development of key features including:

    - Navigation & menus
    - Search functionality
    - Article & news pages
    - Activity calendars
    - Visitor guides
    - Contact forms integrated with **WhatsApp** and other media
  </Card>

  <Card title="Third-Party Integration" color="#ec4899" icon="link">
    Integration with external services such as:

    - **Social media** (Instagram, TikTok, X)
    - Marketplaces or external systems
    - **MOBI Ticketing** for ticket purchases
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

<Tip>
  **User Experience & Visual Design** is implemented across all pages — featuring a modern, clean design consistent with brand guidelines, with an emphasis on ease of navigation, readability, and an optimal user experience.
</Tip>
