## Overview

Display key promotional information such as “Grand Opening Promo 20% Off” as a highlight of the ongoing campaign.

Using engaging and emotional visuals (children with exploration concepts) to strengthen appeal and relevance to target users (families & education).

Presented in slider/carousel form, allowing more than one promo or campaign to be displayed alternately.

Equipped with slider navigation elements (arrows and indicator dots) to move between promo content.

Use a combination of large text (promo headline) and short description as additional explanation.

It has a color overlay to maintain text readability over the background image.

Functions as a bridge from awareness (hero section) to engagement (action or further exploration).

## User Story

<Accordion title="View User Story" defaultOpen>
  - **As a user**, I want to know the latest promotions or offers currently running at Tempe Park.
  - I want to see clear and attractive promotional information so that I am interested in taking advantage of it.
  - I want to be able to easily switch between promotional information if there is more than one offer.
  - **So I can** consider making a visit or purchasing tickets based on the available promotions.
</Accordion>
