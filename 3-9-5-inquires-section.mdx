## Overview

- Displaying the Inquiries section as a means for visitors to contact or establish communication with Tempe Park.
- Provide a title and short description that explains the purpose of the form or contact provided.
- Provides an input form to collect visitor data such as name, email, and message/inquiry.
- Provide structured fields to ensure the information submitted is complete and clear.
- Provides a Submit/Send action button to send inquiries to the system.

## User Story

<Accordion title="View User Story" defaultOpen>
  - **As a visitor**, I would like to contact Tempe Park so I can ask questions or collaborate.
  - **As a visitor**, I would like to fill out the form with my details such as name and email so that Tempe Park can contact me again.
  - **As a visitor**, I want to write down my message or needs so that the information I convey is clear.
  - **As a visitor**, I want to press the submit button so that my inquiry can be sent.
