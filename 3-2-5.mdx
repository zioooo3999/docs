## Overview

- Featuring the main headline: “Find Your Way to Tempe Park with Ease” as an introductory guide to access the location.
- Provides tab-based navigation for various modes of transportation such as Personal Car, Personal Motorcycle, Transjakarta (Bus), and Commuter Line (Train).
- Each tab displays specific information regarding routes, road access, and travel guides according to the selected mode of transportation.
- Provides a list of short information points (highlight points) in the form of a list on the left side to help users understand travel options quickly.
- Displays the main visual (preview image) on the right side to provide an overview of the access conditions or surrounding location.
- Provides additional descriptions explaining trip details, including road conditions, major access points, or nearby landmarks.
- Include a Call-to-Action (CTA) “Buy Ticket” to direct users directly to the ticket purchasing process after determining how to get to the location.
- Designed to help users choose the best mode of transportation and understand travel routes in a practical and informative way.
