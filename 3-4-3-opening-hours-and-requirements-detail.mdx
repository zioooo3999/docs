## Overview

- Displays supporting information in the form of operating hours and visiting requirements for the selected attraction, activity, or facility.
- Provides information on opening hours that are differentiated between weekdays and weekends to provide clarity on visiting times.
- Displays age requirements information to inform you of the age limits or categories that are permitted to participate in activities or access facilities.
- Provide a notes section as additional information that is important or informative for visitors.
- Use a card-shaped layout that is separate from the main content so that information is easy to find and read.

## User Story

<Accordion title="View User Story" defaultOpen>
  - **As a user** who wants to plan a visit, I want to see clear operating hours for weekdays and weekends, so I can determine the appropriate time to visit.
  - **As a user**, I want to know the age requirements of an attraction, activity, or facility so I can determine if the experience is suitable for me or my family.
  - **I would like to** see additional information in the form of notes, so that I don't miss important information before visiting.
</Accordion>
