---
title: "3.13.4. Advisor Section"
---

Overview:

- Displays the Advisor section as information about the parties who guide and support the development of Tempe Park.
- Provides the main heading “We Are Guided by Experienced Minds” to emphasize the credibility of the advisor team.
- Displays a list of advisors in the form of cards containing photos, names, and positions/titles.
- Use a grid layout to arrange multiple advisors neatly and consistently.
- Provide a visual placeholder (profile photo) for each advisor as a representation of identity.

User Story:

- As a visitor, I would like to know who the advisors are from Tempe Park so I can assess their credibility and professionalism.
- As a visitor, I want to see the advisor's name and position so I understand their background.
- As a visitor, I want to see a neat and structured display so that I can easily read the information for each advisor.
- As a visitor, I want to see a photo or visual representation of the advisor to make the information feel more personal and trustworthy
