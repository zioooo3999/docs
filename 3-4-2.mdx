## Overview

- Displays the main detailed content of the attraction, activity, or facility selected by the user in the form of an informative article.
- Provides breadcrumb navigation (Experience \> Detail) to help users understand the position of a page in the website structure.
- Displays a clear and descriptive main title (heading) as a representation of the content being read.
- Presents descriptions in the form of structured text paragraphs to provide complete and in-depth information to users.
- Insert illustrative images in the middle of the content to strengthen visual storytelling and help users imagine the experience more realistically.

## User Story

<Accordion title="View User Story" defaultOpen>
  - **As a user** who wants to know the details of an attraction, activity, or facility, I want to see complete information in structured text form, so I can fully understand the experience offered.
  - **As a user**, I want to see a clear title at the top of the page, so I know the main topic I'm reading about.
  - **As a user**, I want to see breadcrumb navigation, so I can know where I am and return to previous pages easily.
  - **As a user**, I want to see supporting images within the content, so I can imagine the atmosphere or activity being described.
</Accordion>
