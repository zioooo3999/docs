## Overview

The Innovation page showcases various tempeh-based product innovations and developments offered by Tempe Park. This page serves as a product showcase and provides visitors with inspiration for tempeh processing in various modern and creative ways.

The page structure consists of several sections accessible through navigation tabs, including Our Products, Menu Inspiration, Collaboration, and Inquiries. The main content begins with a product catalog with a filter feature, followed by visually appealing tempeh-based menu inspiration. This page is designed to provide a product exploration experience while also educating about modern tempeh innovations.
