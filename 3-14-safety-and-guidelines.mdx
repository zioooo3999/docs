The Safety & Guidelines page provides information on the rules, safety guidelines, and regulations that visitors should adhere to while at Tempe Park. This page aims to create a safe, comfortable, and enjoyable visitor experience for all visitors, while ensuring that all activities and facilities are used properly and in accordance with applicable regulations

<Frame>
  ![Safety & Guidelines Cut](/images/Safety-&-Guidelines-Cut.png)
</Frame>

## Overview

- Displays safety information and visiting guidelines that all visitors must adhere to while at Tempe Park.
- Provides information regarding visitor responsibilities, restricted areas, and potential risks to be aware of during a visit.
- Displays security policies, baggage checks, and a list of items that are not allowed into the Tempe Park area.
- Provides information regarding assistance procedures, emergency contacts, and steps to take in certain conditions.
- Help create a safe, comfortable and orderly environment for all visitors by conveying clear rules and regulations.
- Display information in a structured and easy-to-read format so visitors can quickly understand the visiting guide.

## User Story

  - **As a visitor**, I want to see clear safety information and visitor guidelines so I understand the rules that apply while I'm at Tempe Park.
