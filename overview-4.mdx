## Overview

The News Article page is an aggregated content page featuring articles and insights accessible via the "View All News" CTA on the homepage. This page serves as an information hub, presenting various articles related to Tempe Park, covering topics such as education, culture, innovation, and sustainability.

Structurally, this page begins with a highlights section, a card slider, displaying featured articles in a large, visual format to capture users' attention. Below that is the Other Insights section, which lists articles in a list format with categories, search, and pagination features to facilitate broader content exploration.
