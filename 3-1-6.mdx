## Overview

- Featuring a section headlined “Stories That Grow From Our Park” as a platform for the latest articles and news from Tempe Park.
- Presenting content in the form of a card layout consisting of several of the latest articles.
- Each card displays elements such as a cover image, article category, title, short description, and a “Read More” CTA.
- Using visuals that are relevant to the themes of education, nature, and tempeh to strengthen the context of the content.
- Provides a main “View All News” button to direct users to the complete article list page.
- The content structure is designed to make it easier for users to scan the available articles.
- Functions as a medium to convey the latest information, education, and storytelling that strengthens engagement with users.

## User Story

<Accordion title="View User Story" defaultOpen>
