## Overview

- Displays breadcrumb navigation (Article \> Detail Article) to help users understand the page position.
- Provide a clear and prominent article title as the main focus of the page.
- Displays article metadata information such as publication date.
- Provide a hero image or featured image that represents the content of the article.
- Displays article content in the form of structured and easy-to-read text paragraphs.
- Using a single main column layout to increase focus and reading comfort.
- Provides a share feature (social media) on the right side to make it easier for users to share articles.

## User Story

<Accordion title="View User Story" defaultOpen>
  - **As a user** who wants to read the complete article, I want to see the content of the article clearly structured, so that I can understand the information conveyed easily.
  - **As a user**, I want to see the article title and publication date,
  - **So that** I know the context and relevance of the information.
  - **As a user**, I want to see the main image in an article, so I get a visual representation of the content of the article.
  - **As a user**, I want to read articles with a neat and comfortable layout, so that I don't feel tired when reading long content.
</Accordion>
