## Overview

- Displaying the headline or title: "Explore Nature, Culture, and Creation" which explains that Tempe Park is a nature and culture-based tourist destination.
- Contains a brief description of Tempe Park as a destination that combines nature, culture, and innovation in one integrated experience.
- Presenting two main CTAs, namely “Buy REGULAR TICKET” and “Buy Group Ticket” to accommodate the needs of individual and group users.
- Displays additional navigation elements in the form of a floating “Ticket” shortcut to facilitate quick access to the ticket purchasing process.
- Using a two-column layout (visual and content) to maintain a balance between visual storytelling and functional information.
