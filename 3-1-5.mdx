## Overview

- Featuring sections that focus on Tempe Park's values ​​and commitment to aspects such as sustainability, education, and social impact.
- Using a visual approach in the form of an image grid/card layout that represents real activities or initiatives (e.g. education, community, environment).
- Each card displays a visual activity accompanied by a short description to explain the form of commitment made.
- Using human-centric visual elements (human activity, interaction, learning) to strengthen emotional messages and credibility.
- Utilize overlays or visual layering to maintain aesthetic consistency with other sections.
- Designed to support brand storytelling, Tempe Park is not just a tourist destination, but also has greater value and purpose.
- Functions as a brand trust booster and differentiation for Tempe Park compared to other tourist destinations.
