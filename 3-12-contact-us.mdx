The Contact Us page provides a way for visitors to contact Tempe Park directly. This page is designed to facilitate communication between users and management, whether for questions, collaborations, or further information regarding available services and programs

<Frame>
  ![Contact Us Cut](/images/Contact-Us-Cut.png)
</Frame>

## Overview

- Displays a hero section with the title “Contact Us” as a page introduction.
- Provides a two-column layout consisting of a visual image on the left side and a contact form on the right side.
- Showcasing images of visitor activity at Tempe Park to create a warm, friendly, and interactive experience.
- Provides a contact form consisting of several fields such as name, email, telephone number, question topic, and message.
- Provide input fields with placeholders to guide users in filling in data.
- Provide a dropdown in the inquiry topics section to make it easier for users to select question categories.
- Displays a “Submit” call-to-action button to send the completed form.

## User Story

<Accordion title="View User Story" defaultOpen>
  - **As a visitor**, I would like to see a contact page so I can contact Tempe Park easily.
  - **As a visitor**, I would like to fill out a form with data such as name, email, and message so that I can convey my questions or needs.
  - **As a visitor**, I want to choose the topic of my question so that my message can be handled more appropriately.
  - **As a visitor**, I want to see a form display that is clear and easy to understand so that I don't have difficulty filling in the data.
  - **As a visitor**, I want to press the submit button so that my message can be sent to the relevant party.
</Accordion>
