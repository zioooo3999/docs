## Overview

- Displays the Related section as a recommendation for other content that is still relevant to the attraction, activity, or facility that the user is currently viewing.
- Provide clear section titles (e.g. “Related Activities”) to indicate the relationship to the main content.
- Displays a list of content in the form of visual cards containing images, item names, and a “Learn More” CTA.
- Use a horizontal layout (grid or carousel) to display multiple recommendations at once.
- Presenting engaging and contextual visuals to encourage further exploration by users.

## User Story

<Accordion title="View User Story" defaultOpen>
  - **As a user** looking at details about an attraction, activity, or facility, I want to see recommendations for other relevant content, so I can discover other experiences that might be of interest to me.
  - **As a user**, I want to see recommendations in the form of attractive visual cards, so I can quickly understand and compare the available options.
  - **As a user**, I want to press the “Learn More” button on one of the recommendations, so I can go directly to the detail page of that content.
  - **As a user**, I would like to have the option to view all experiences via the “Explore All Experiences” button, so I can go back and explore the entire Experience page.
</Accordion>
