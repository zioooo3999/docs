## Overview

The Education page is one of the main pages accessible through the Tempe Park website's navbar. It's designed as an educational information center, covering various aspects of tempeh, from its history and interesting facts to its cultural value and benefits. The page structure is divided into several main categories, such as History of Tempe, Fun Facts, Tempe Warrior & Hero, and School Programs, which can be accessed through tabbed navigation. This approach allows users to explore educational content in a focused and organized manner.
