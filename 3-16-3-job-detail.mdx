## Overview

- Displays detailed information about the selected job vacancy, including position name, location, candidate category, and job level.
- Provide a job description, key responsibilities, and requirements the candidate must meet.
- Displays information on facilities and benefits provided to employees.
- Provides an Apply button to apply for jobs and a Share feature to share vacancy information.
- Displays breadcrumb navigation to help users find their page position and return to previous pages.

## User Story

<Accordion title="View User Story" defaultOpen>
  - **As a job seeker**, I want to see complete information about a vacancy so I can understand the position being offered.
  - **As a job seeker**, I want to know the duties and requirements of the job so I can assess the suitability of my qualifications.
  - **As a job seeker**, I want to see the benefits provided so that I understand the benefits that will be gained.
  - **As a job seeker**, I want to share vacancy information with others so they can be aware of available career opportunities.
  - **As a job seeker**, I want to apply for jobs directly through the details page so that the registration process becomes easier and faster.
</Accordion>
