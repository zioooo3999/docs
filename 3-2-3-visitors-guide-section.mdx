## Overview

- Featuring the main headline: “Everything You Need for Your Visit” as part of a comprehensive visiting guide.
- Using tab-based navigation (Operating Hours, Daily Schedule, Parking & Direction, Tempe Park Map, Accessibility) with an active focus on the Tempe Park Map.
- Presenting a visual map of the Tempe Park area in the form of an aerial view illustration that provides a comprehensive overview of the area and facilities.
- Displays several interactive points (map markers) that represent important locations within the Tempe Park area.
- Allows users to visually understand the layout of the area, distribution of facilities, and exploration paths before visiting.
- Using a visual-first approach to improve ease of navigation and help users orient themselves within the area.
