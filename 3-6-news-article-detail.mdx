## Overview

The News Article Detail page is a follow-up page that appears when a user selects an article from the News Articles page. This page provides complete and in-depth information about the article.

The page structure is designed with a focus on content readability, starting with the article title, publication information, and the main visual, all the way through to the article's content in structured paragraphs. Furthermore, supporting elements include breadcrumb navigation for user orientation and a share feature for sharing articles across various platforms.
