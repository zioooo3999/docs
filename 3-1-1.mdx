## Overview

The Hero Section serves as the primary entry point for the Tempe Park website, designed to create a strong first impression and immediately communicate the brand's core identity.

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Main Headline" color="#10b981" icon="heading">
    **“A Fun Way to Learn, Play, and Taste Indonesia’s Heritage”** — reinforces Tempe Park’s positioning as a culture- and experience-based edu-park destination.
  </Card>

  <Card title="Hero Visual" color="#38bdf8" icon="image">
    An aerial view of the Tempe Park area depicting the natural environment, modern facilities, and overall guest experience as a whole.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

### Key Interface Elements

- **Narrative Description:** A concise explanation introducing tempeh's journey from its traditional origins to a global product through an immersive experience.
- **Dual Call-to-Action (CTA):**
  - **Primary Action:** `Buy Ticket` — Direct conversion path for park visitors.
  - **Secondary Action:** `Learn More` — Exploratory path for additional details.
- **Visual Layering:** Uses overlay elements and subtle graphic shapes to enhance aesthetic appeal and focus attention on the primary copy.
- **Top Navigation Bar:**
  - **Core Links:** `Home`, `Plan Your Visit`, `Experience`, `Education`, and `Innovation`.
  - **Utilities:** Integrated Language Switcher and Search functionality.

## User Story

<Accordion title="View User Persona & Goals" defaultOpen>
  **As a first-time visitor to the site:**

  - I want to immediately understand what Tempe Park is and what it has to offer.
  - I want a professional and engaging first impression that inspires me to explore further.
  - I want to easily locate key actions like purchasing tickets or gathering detailed information.

  **Outcome:** I feel motivated to visit Tempe Park and continue exploring other sections of the platform.
</Accordion>
