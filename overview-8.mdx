## Overview

The Career Page displays a list of available job openings at Tempe Park. This page is designed to help prospective applicants find suitable positions based on keywords, job level, job type, and location. Users can view brief information about each available position, learn more about the job opening, and apply directly through the website.

The page structure consists of several sections designed to streamline the recruitment process, including job search filters, candidate qualification details, and direct application pathways. This page aims to deliver an efficient job discovery experience for applicants while maintaining clear brand representation for Tempe Park's recruitment team.
