## Overview

- Featuring the Foundation section as the main introduction to the background and concept of Tempe Park.
- Provides the main headline “A Living Journey of Tempe and Nature” to convey the brand values ​​and identity.
- Features a narrative description explaining the philosophy, location, and purpose of Tempe Park.
- Using a combination layout between visuals (images) and text to increase user appeal and understanding.
- Displaying representative visuals in the form of individuals and activities related to tempeh and education.

## User Story

<Accordion title="View User Story" defaultOpen>
  - **As a visitor**, I want to understand the background of Tempe Park so that I know the purpose and concept of the place.
  - **As a visitor**, I want to read a clear explanation of Tempe Park's philosophy so that I will be more interested and trust the brand.
  - **As a visitor**, I want to see relevant visuals so I can imagine the experience on offer.
  - **As a visitor**, I would like to watch related videos so I can get a more realistic picture of the activities at Tempe Park.
