## Overview

- Featuring a section with the headline “In The Beginning” as part of Tempe Park’s storytelling journey.
- Using the main visual in the form of an aerial landscape of Tempe Park to provide a comprehensive overview of the area and environment.
- Contains a short description explaining the exploration concepts and experiences that can be found in every corner of Tempe Park.
- There is a main Call-to-Action (CTA) namely “Explore” which directs users to explore more deeply regarding the journey or experience.
- Equipped with a horizontal slider (carousel) at the bottom that displays several locations or experience points.
- Each item in the slider contains an image of the location, the name of the place, and a CTA “View This Place”.
- It has slider navigation in the form of arrows and progress indicators to make it easier to move between content.
- Use a visual overlay to maintain text readability over a background image.
- Functions as a storytelling medium that connects brand concepts with real experiences on location.

## User Story

<Accordion title="View User Story" defaultOpen>
  - **As a user**, I want to understand the journey or main concept of Tempe Park so I know what makes this place unique.
  - I wanted to see a broad and engaging visual overview of the Tempe Park area to build expectations before visiting.
  - I want to be able to explore different locations or experience points easily through a slider.
  - I would like to be directed to more detailed information about a particular place that interests me.
