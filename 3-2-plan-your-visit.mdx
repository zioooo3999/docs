## Overview

The Tempe Park homepage is designed as a primary entry point, representing the brand's identity while providing a strong initial user experience. It combines an immersive visual approach with a user-journey-driven content structure to introduce Tempe Park as an edu-park destination that integrates education, recreation, and cultural exploration.

The front page of **Tempe Park's newly revamped website** is designed to deliver a strong, modern first-person experience that aligns with Tempe Park's identity as an edu-park destination based on nature, culture, and innovation. The front page serves not only as a homepage but also as a brand gateway, visually and narratively showcasing the essence of Tempe Park.

Through an immersive visual approach, the use of large-scale imagery, and a user-journey-based content structure, this page successfully communicates Tempe Park's core values as an integrated space for learning, recreation, and exploration. Elements such as a hero section with strong storytelling, highlight experiences, and programs and articles are designed to guide users from awareness to engagement.

## User Journey Flow

The page layout guides visitors through a clear funnel structure:

<Steps>
  <Step title="1. Awareness (Hero Section)">
    Captures immediate interest through high-impact imagery and strong brand storytelling.
  </Step>
  <Step title="2. Exploration (Highlight Experiences)">
    Showcases key attraction zones, experiential activities, and park highlights.
  </Step>
  <Step title="3. Engagement (Programs & Articles)">
    Connects visitors with ongoing activities, news, educational content, and events.
  </Step>
</Steps>
