## Welcome to the Tempe Park CMS

This guide is designed for **Super Admin** users to navigate and manage the content of the Tempe Park Website efficiently using the Strapi headless CMS. It provides step-by-step instructions on accessing the system, managing various collections, and updating website pages.

### Collection Types

The CMS is divided into various **Collection Types** (Modules) that control different sections of the website. This guide covers how to manage:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Activities & Attractions">
    Manage the main attractions, rides, and activities available at Tempe Park.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Events & Calendars">
    Update schedules, upcoming events, and operational calendars.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Facilities">
    Maintain information about park facilities, dining, and amenities.
  </Step>
  <Step title="News & Articles">
    Publish and organize news updates and informational articles.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Products & FAQ">
    Manage merchandise, ticketing packages, and frequently asked questions.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Dynamic Pages">
    Construct and edit dynamic pages across the website using flexible content blocks.
  </Step>
</Steps>
