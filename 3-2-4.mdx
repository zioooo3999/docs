## Overview

- Featuring the main headline: “Explore Events and Activities at Tempe Park” as an introduction to exploring the available activity schedule.
- Provides a day-based calendar view (weekly view) that displays scheduled activities from Monday to Sunday.
- Each day displays a list of activities with time, activity name (e.g. Guided Park Exploration Tour), and location.
- Activities are displayed in a structured card format so they are easy to scan and compare across time and days.
- Provides visual highlights on specific days or activities to help users focus on the currently selected or recommended schedule.
- Designed to provide a complete overview of the experiences visitors can take part in while at Tempe Park.
- Supports visit planning by allowing users to see the distribution of activity throughout the day and choose the best time.
